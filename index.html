STEP 7D Â· SCALING IN & SCALING OUT

Scaling In & Scaling Out (Position Management)

Scaling In and Scaling Out are advanced trade management techniques used to maximize profits and reduce risk.

What is Scaling In?

Scaling In means adding more position to a winning trade. You add only when the trade is already in profit and the market confirms your direction.

  • Add only to winning trades
  • Never add to a losing trade
  • Add after confirmation (break & retest, structure shift)

What is Scaling Out?

Scaling Out means booking profit in parts instead of closing the entire trade at once. This helps protect profits and control emotions.

  • Book partial profit at key levels
  • Reduce emotional pressure
  • Let remaining position run with trailing stop-loss

Simple Example

A trade is entered at support. Price moves in your favor and breaks resistance. A small additional position is added (Scaling In). Partial profits are booked at higher resistance levels (Scaling Out).

Golden Rule:
Scale In only on winning trades.
Scale Out to protect profits.
